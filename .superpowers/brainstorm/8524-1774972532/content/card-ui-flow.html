Card Types by Block

Warm-Up Cards

Format: Multiple choice (tappable option cards)

Style: Fun, emoji-decorated, 3-4 options

Feel: "Pick one — no wrong answers!"

5 questions, non-scoring, builds comfort

RIASEC Interest Cards

Format: 5-point Likert slider

Style: Activity description + "How much would you enjoy this?"

Scale: 😒 Hate it → 😐 Neutral → 🤩 Love it

18 questions, one RIASEC type each

Personality Pair Cards

Format: Forced-choice spectrum slider

Style: "Which sounds more like you?" with A ← slider → B

Feel: Two options, slide toward the one that fits

8 questions, 2 per MBTI dichotomy

Profile Reveal Cards

Format: Chart reveal + confirmatory Likert questions

Style: "Your profile is taking shape!" + targeted follow-ups

Feel: Achievement moment with refinement

5 adaptive questions from pool of 18

Transitions Between Cards

Within Block

Slide left/right animation. Next card slides in from right, previous exits left. Progress bar fills smoothly.

Between Blocks

Brief interstitial card: "Nice! Now let's explore your interests..." with block pill updating. Fade transition.

Chart Unlock

After Block 2: RIASEC hexagon animates in from center. After Block 3: MBTI sliders slide in from edges. Celebration particles.
