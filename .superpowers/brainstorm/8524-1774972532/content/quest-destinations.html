How Ranked Destinations Are Used

During the Quest
  • #1 Primary: Gets the most detailed programme matching and scholarship search
  • #2 Backup: Gets programme suggestions and entry requirements comparison
  • #3 Safety: Gets basic programme options and "compared to #1" analysis
  • All 3 appear in the final report side-by-side
